This is probably the most useful page to me on the entire site. Ever since I was a kid I've read a lot, but I've never ever kept track of all the books I read. It helps that now I buy every book I read, so I have a physical record of all the books, but this model gets less reliable when you consider how often people lose stuff and the fact that I own a lot of books that I've never read.

So here I'll keep a running record of the books I read every year, I figure it might also be interesting to see how many books/pages I end up reading and if I'm getting any faster or slower. There is a separate section for audiobooks (I lied earlier...) since including those in a real book list is cheating big time. Under my listening lists I'll also include any podcasts of significant length, i.e. Hardcore History.
