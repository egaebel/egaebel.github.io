Hi, I'm Ethan, and a few weeks ago I decided it was time to join the world of the interwebs and make my own website. Given that this opportunity has been around for literally my entire life I'm only about 9727 days (or 26.6 years) late to the party.

So, what can I say here? I'm a computer scientist currently working at Google on Cloud Security and I've been working there for (2016/08/01 - currentDate = ) about a year and a month (note to self, add some fancy Javascript to this page). Before that I worked on my bachelors and masters degrees in compute science at Virginia Tech. During my masters I worked in the Complex Networks and Security Research lab on security issues in spectrum sharing, a new and more intuitive authentication scheme for augmented reality, and training deep learning models with user data without revealing the user data to the entity training the model. Besides a degree I published a chapter in a book on spectrum sharing and published a paper on the augmented reality authentication scheme at the 6th International Workshop on Trustworthy Embeded Devices (TrustED) in 2016 in Vienna, Austria.

When I'm not at work making the cloud more secure I'm usually reading books, listening to books on tape (I refuse to call them audiobooks...), watching way too many shows, hiking, or reading papers on natural lanugage processing, machine learning, security, or privacy (which is usually negatively impacted by advances in the first two areas).
